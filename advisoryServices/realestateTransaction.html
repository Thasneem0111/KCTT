Real Estate Transaction

Witnessing and verification of purchase & sale agreements and mortgage documents for Canadian properties.

Real estate transactions involve multiple legal documents that must be properly witnessed and verified to hold validity in Canada. We assist expats by reviewing, witnessing, and authenticating purchase and sale agreements, as well as mortgage contracts. This service ensures compliance with Canadian real estate regulations and protects buyers and sellers from legal complications. With our support, clients can complete transactions remotely while maintaining full legal security.

Our team provides professional witnessing for Canadian property agreements. We help expats avoid errors and ensure all real estate documents are enforceable in Canada.
