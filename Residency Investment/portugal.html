Portugal Residency By Investment

The Portugal Golden Visa program offers investors a pathway to European residency and citizenship through diverse investment options. Starting from EUR 200,000 in cultural projects, or EUR 500,000 in business, research, or venture capital funds, applicants can secure residency for themselves and their families. The program is flexible, family-friendly, and provides access to Portugal’s thriving economy, high quality of life, and visa-free travel across the EU Schengen Zone and beyond.

The Portugal Golden Visa program offers multiple qualifying investment routes. Capital transfer options include: EUR 500,000 in investment or venture capital funds supporting Portuguese companies; EUR 500,000 in research activities (reduced to EUR 400,000 in low-density areas); or EUR 250,000 in artistic or cultural heritage projects (reduced to EUR 200,000 in low-density areas). The business route requires either the creation of at least 10 jobs (8 in low-density areas) or an investment of EUR 500,000 in a company incorporated and headquartered in Portugal, creating or maintaining a minimum of 5–10 permanent jobs for a period of 3 years. Low-density areas are defined as regions with fewer than 100 inhabitants per km² or GDP per capita below 75% of the national average. Residency is initially valid for 2 years, renewable, with eligibility for permanent residency and citizenship after 5 years, subject to meeting legal and language requirements.

Residency Planning

Portugal Residency Overview

The Portugal Golden Visa program is a residence-by-investment initiative designed to attract global investors through capital transfer or business creation. It provides a pathway for non-EU nationals and their families to secure residency in Portugal, with access to Europe’s Schengen area and eligibility for citizenship after 5 years of legal residence.

Why Portugal?

Portugal residency is highly attractive because it combines flexible investment options with one of the shortest timeframes in Europe to citizenship. Investors benefit from a stable EU economy, high quality of life, access to world-class healthcare and education, and visa-free travel across the Schengen zone. The program also supports family inclusion and offers investment reductions in designated low-density regions.

Key Benefits

  • Residency permit valid for 2 years, renewable, with a path to permanent residency
  • Eligibility for citizenship and a Portuguese passport after 5 years of legal residence
  • Visa-free travel within Europe’s Schengen area
  • Flexible investment options starting from EUR 200,000 in low-density areas
  • Options include capital transfer (funds, research, culture) or business/job creation
  • Full family inclusion: spouse, children, and dependent parents
  • Access to Portugal’s healthcare, education, and business environment
  • Low-density regions offer reduced investment thresholds
