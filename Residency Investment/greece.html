Greece Residency By Investment

Greece offers a highly sought-after Residency by Investment Program, allowing investors to secure permanent residency through real estate investments. The program features flexible investment thresholds starting from EUR 250,000 for specific projects, EUR 400,000 in most regions, and EUR 800,000 in prime areas such as Athens, Thessaloniki, Mykonos, and Santorini. Residency permits are valid for 5 years and renewable, with eligibility for citizenship after 7 years. Families benefit from healthcare, education, and visa-free travel across the EU Schengen Zone.

From September 1, 2024, new investment thresholds apply: EUR 800,000 in Attica (Athens region), Thessaloniki, Mykonos, Santorini, and islands with populations over 3,100, and EUR 400,000 in other areas of Greece. Reduced investment of EUR 250,000 applies to commercial-to-residential conversions or restoration of historic buildings. Investors must acquire a single property of at least 120 sqm (unless under the exceptions). The application process typically takes 30–90 days and can be completed via power of attorney through the Greek Embassy. Once approved, the investor and family receive renewable 5-year residency permits with full access to healthcare, education, and visa-free Schengen travel. Citizenship and a Greek passport are available after 7 years, subject to language and cultural knowledge requirements.

Greece Residency Overview

The Greece Golden Visa program, launched in 2014, is a residence-by-investment initiative that allows non-EU nationals and their families to obtain permanent resident permits in Greece and the wider European Union. It offers a straightforward pathway to European residency through real estate investment.

Why Greece?

Greece residency is attractive because it provides a secure and affordable entry into the European Union, with the ability to live in a country rich in culture, history, and lifestyle. It also ensures access to the Schengen zone, public services, and future eligibility for EU citizenship.

Key Benefits

  • Permanent Residency (PR) granted for 5 years, renewable every 5 years
  • Free travel within the Schengen area
  • No minimum stay requirement in Greece
  • Free access to public healthcare in Greece
  • Right to work in Europe and establish a business in Greece
  • Residency covers full family members, including children up to 21 years and parents of both spouses
  • Eligibility to apply for citizenship after 7 years
