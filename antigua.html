Antigua & Barbuda Citizenship by Investment

Antigua & Barbuda offers one of the Caribbean’s most flexible Citizenship by Investment Programs, with options starting from USD 230,000. Successful applicants and their families gain citizenship and a passport that provides visa-free or visa-on-arrival access to over 157 countries, including the UK, Schengen states, and many Commonwealth nations. The program is family-friendly, cost-effective, and designed for global mobility and lifestyle freedom.
