Canada Immigration Programs

Explore our comprehensive range of Canadian immigration programs designed to help you achieve permanent residency through various pathways.

Spouse & Common-law Sponsorship

Sponsorship program for Canadians married to or in a common-law relationship with a non-Canadian spouse.

Key Features

  • Sponsor spouse or common-law partner
  • Family reunification program
  • Option for inland or outland sponsorship
  • Open work permit during process
  • Pathway to permanent residence
+2 more features

Requirements

Sponsor must be Canadian Citizen/PR, prove genuine relationship, and meet financial and residency requirements.

Permanent Residence Programs

Obtain Canadian permanent residence under various immigration pathways.

Key Features

  • Express Entry system
  • Skilled Worker programs
  • Canadian Experience Class
  • Provincial Nominee Program
  • Family Sponsorship
+2 more features

Requirements

Meet eligibility criteria such as work experience, education, and language proficiency depending on the program.

Start-up Visa

Immigration program for innovative entrepreneurs launching businesses in Canada.

Key Features

  • Support from designated organizations
  • Business incubator or accelerator approval
  • Angel investor or venture capital backing
  • Pathway to permanent residency
  • Access to Canadian markets
+2 more features

Requirements

Innovative business concept, proof of designated organization support, and sufficient settlement funds.
